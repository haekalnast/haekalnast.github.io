B2B Disbursement Dashboard Design

Overview

Dipay Disbursement is a revolutionary B2B disbursement platform by PT. Marco Prima Mandiri (Surya Fajar Group). It simplifies bulk transactions and electronic payments for large-scale businesses, providing a seamless and efficient solution.

As the Lead Product Designer, my mission was to craft an intuitive, powerful design that simplifies bulk disbursements. I collaborated with a skilled team of designers and product managers to realize this vision.

Problem

Large-scale businesses face challenges in managing bulk transactions efficiently and securely. They need a robust platform that offers clear, actionable insights and ease of use.

Goals

  • Streamline bulk financial transactions.
  • Design a robust and easy-to-navigate interface.
  • Empower businesses with comprehensive transaction insights.

Approach

Using secondary research, we identified the needs and preferences of large businesses. We developed wireframes, user flows, and high-fidelity prototypes. Through extensive usability testing, we refined every aspect to perfection.

Outcome

The new design of Dipay Disbursement is transformative. It has increased transaction efficiency, improved security measures, and enhanced user satisfaction, redefining how businesses manage their finances.
